Welcome to the Pollution Traffic Weather Project

Short into

On this site you can access and explore weather, traffic and air polution data for the Bergen Area. To see some cool graphs, go to the EXPLORE tab. Our data is stored as RDF graphs. To query the data as a SPARQL endpoint, go to the QUERY tab. If you want to know more about our project, go to the about tab

About us

We are a group of three bachelor level students at the Department of Information Science and Media Studies, University of Bergen. Our goal was to develop a web service that makes air pollution- traffic- and weather data available for exploration by anyone interested, and SPARQL query by anyone capable. Increasing awareness of and levels of air pollution in cities call for insight into how weather and traffic interacts with air pollution levels. Historical data for Norway is only available upon request. Our idea was that making the data more available and public will make it more useful.

Explore

If you click on the EXPLORE tab, you wil see several sgvizler graphs, accompanied by the SPARQL queries that were used to retrieve the RDF graphs that are visualized. NB: The visualizations might take a while to load. You can hover over each bar or pie slice in the graph to see more information about it.
